불고기 ~ Bulgogi

Bulgogi is a popular Korean dish consisting of thinly sliced beef marinated in a sweet and savory soy sauce mixture and grilled or stir-fried until tender.

김치찌개 ~ Kimchi-jjigae

Kimchi-jjigae is a hearty, savory, and spicy Korean stew made by simmering aged kimchi with pork, tofu, and aromatics, serving as a staple comfort food.

초계국수 ~ Chogye-guksu

Chogye-guksu is a refreshing Korean summer noodle dish featuring buckwheat noodles served in a tangy, icy chicken broth topped with shredded chicken, mustard, and vinegar.
