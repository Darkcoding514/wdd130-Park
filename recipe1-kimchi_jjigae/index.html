Kimchi Jjigae

Ingredients

Beef broth: 1L; Kimchi: 1 cabbage; Pork belly: 400g; Ground red pepper: 3tbsp; fish sauce: 3tbsp; Beef bouillon: 1tbsp; Minced garlic: 1tbsp; Water if needed; Tofu: as much as wanted; Green onion: 200g.

Instructions

This is actually an easy recipe. Put everything and then boil it until the meat is cooked.
