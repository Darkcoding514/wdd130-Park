Gooksu

Ingredients

Zucchini: 700g (cut into long pieces); Salt: 1tbsp; Cooking oil; Salted shrimp: 0.5tbsp; Noodles: as much as you want; Water : 1L; Seafood Dashi pack: 1pack; Soy sauce: 1tbsp; Fish sauce: 1tbsp; Crushed seaweed: a handful; Red pepper: to taste; Toasted Sesame seed: a shake; Crushed red pepper: to taste.

Instructions

(1) Put zucchini and salt together. Pour away the water as it comes out. (2) Put salted zucchini, salted shrimp on oiled pan and stir fry it. (3) Now it is a broth part. Put water and Dashi pack together and boil it. (4) When it is boiling, put fish sauce and soy sauce. (5) Cook noodles. (6) Now in a bowl put noodles, broth, zucchini, crushed seaweed, red pepper, crushed red pepper, toasted sesame seed.
